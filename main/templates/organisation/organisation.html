Organisation Summary

This page provides a department-level overview of the SkyHub organisation. It helps users understand how teams are grouped, who leads each department, and which teams may need management attention.

The page includes filtering, sorting and analytics features, allowing users to identify high-risk departments and monitor team status more effectively.

The page is dynamically connected to the shared database models, so changes made through the admin panel or other modules are reflected automatically.
